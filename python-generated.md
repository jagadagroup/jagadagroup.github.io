# Exporting Pyrotechnic Metal Powders from India: Critical Insights

India is an emerging leader in the export of pyrotechnic-grade metal powders, including **magnalium**, **magnesium**, and **aluminium-based alloys**. These powders find extensive applications in fireworks, aerospace, automotive industries, and specialty chemicals. Here's a comprehensive overview for buyers exploring India's export market.

### Key Pyrotechnic Metal Powders from India

**1. Magnalium Powder**
- Characteristics: Aluminium-magnesium alloy known for its lightweight properties.
- Common Ratios: 50:50, 60:40 alloy blends.
- Applications: Pyrotechnics, fireworks, and metallurgy.

**2. Magnesium Powder**
- Characteristics: Highly reactive.
- Uses: Military-grade flares, fireworks, desulfurization in chemical industries, and Grignard reagents.
